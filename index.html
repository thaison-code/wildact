WILDACT – WE ACT FOR WILDLIFE

Established in 2015, WildAct is a non-profit organisation committed to wildlife conservation and protection in Vietnam. Aiming to “Investing in people for a thriving planet”, WildAct works to create lasting change through community-driven initiatives.

With the slogan “We Act for Wildlife”, WildAct is dedicated to taking real action and making a tangible impact – both for wildlife and the people who share their environment.

 Conservation is not just a scientific effort – it is a journey of love and deep respect for nature and all living beings. This website is a hub to tell our story, we hope to inspire you, spark your passion, and share the driving force behind WildAct’s relentless dedication and efforts.

LOCAL & ETHNIC MINORITY EMPOWERMENT

By empowering local communities, WildAct transforms those closest to the problem into key players in the solution.

WOMEN’S LEADERSHIP & EQUITY

WildAct fosters gender equity by enabling conditions for women to lead in conservation, breaking barriers in traditionally male-dominated spaces.

EDUCATION

Through workshops, school programmes, and hands-on activities, WildAct nurtures the next generation of conservationists, fostering a lifelong commitment to protecting biodiversity.
