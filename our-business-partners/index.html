A PIVOTAL MOMENT FOR WILDLIFE CONSERVATION

We are at a critical turning point in nature.

Across the globe, species are vanishing at an alarming rate – deforestation wipes out habitats, illegal wildlife
trade pushes species to the brink, and human-wildlife conflict worsens. In Southeast Asia alone, over 25% of
species face extinction, including the large-antlered muntjac, pangolins, and migratory birds struggling to
survive. In Vietnam, it was estimated more than 200,000 snares exist in protected areas.

At WildAct, we are committed to a future where people and wildlife thrive together – but we can’t do it alone.

That’s why we’re calling on businesses and companies to be part of the solution. Together, we can make a real
impact for wildlife and the planet.

PARTNERING FOR A SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

At WildAct, we understand the challenges businesses face in balancing growth with sustainability. But we also know that a successful business depends on a thriving planet. That’s why we work with companies to integrate high-impact conservation solutions into their operations – creating real value for both nature and business.

By partnering with us, your business can become a driving force for sustainability.

ALIGN WITH UNITED NATIONS
SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS (SDGs)

WildAct’s activities align with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by integrating education, gender equality, biodiversity conservation, climate action, and partnerships:

QUALITY EDUCATION

We provide training, scholarships, and workshops to equip young people, local communities, and conservationists with essential skills.

GENDER EQUALITY

Our programmes empower women and ethnic minorities, ensuring inclusivity in conservation efforts.

REDUCED INEQUALITIES

We support underrepresented groups by creating opportunities for participation in environmental protection.

CLIMATE ACTION

Through forest patrols and habitat restoration, we help mitigate climate change and strengthen resilience.

LIFE ON LAND

Our conservation initiatives protect endangered species and restore ecosystems.

PARTNERSHIP FOR THE GOALS

We collaborate with local and international partners to maximise conservation impact.
